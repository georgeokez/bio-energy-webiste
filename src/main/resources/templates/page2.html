About Us

Eongratis limited is a renewable company base in Lagos, we provide cutting edge custom energy facilities in strategic locations in Lagos Nigeria. We focus on providing Bioenergy and Hybrid energy technology to our clients all over Nigeria.
Our Bioenergy facilities focus on harnessing the potential of organic waste and energy crop plantation. Our Organic waste disposal service provides waste generators such as food processors, food retailers, waste haulers, abattoirs and any business where food is either prepared or consumed. Our Hybrid technology seek to tap into the potential of solar and pump storage technology that best fit the environment requirement of our clients
With the focus on providing contemporary sustainable and clean energy facilities for our clients we seek to contribute to a green environment in Nigeria.
