Captive Power

Commercial establishment are constantly seeking for alternative power to efficiently manage their resources.  

Our captive power facility have the potential to transform energy consumption into efficient and sustainable energy. We provide off grid power plant that allows commercial establishment to efficiently manage their energy through clean energy alternative power supply.

Our power generation alternative provides clean energy that maximizes the value of Bioenergy by harnessing its methane content while also recapturing and returning valuable nutrients as an organic fertilizer and Hybrid technology by using natural source of power generation through energy from the sun and environment with high elevation.
